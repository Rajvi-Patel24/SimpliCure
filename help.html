Help Center

How do I report an issue with a water source?

On the Clean Water page, you can use the "Share Your Experience" form to report issues or provide feedback on any listed water source.

Is the waste pickup service available everywhere?

Availability depends on your location. Please enter your address in the scheduling form to see if the service is offered in your area.

How is the water quality data collected?

We work with local health departments and certified labs to gather and display the latest water quality test results.
